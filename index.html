DK!SONICTALE

DK!SONICTALE is an UNDERTALE AU that places Sonic the Hedgehog characters into UNDERTALE's setting, keeping their personalities, histories, and beliefs intact while exploring them in a new context. Inspiration has been taken from UNDERTOAD, an AU created by LucasPucas and co-created by Starfury and Patwhit01 that blends Super Mario characters with UNDERTALE in a very unique way.

Due to the many different portrayals of Sonic the Hedgehog over the years, this AU is a composite of each of them, blending every single interpretation of the franchise that I could find into one unique package. The planet is called Earth, the characters are called Mobians, and various obscure Sonic characters have the potential to show up in this story.

The story tackles the uprooting of one's history, and the many coping mechanisms that we use to distract ourselves from the pain of loss. If you've ever moved frequently as a child or had your childhood uprooted, you know all too well the themes this story will be tackling...
