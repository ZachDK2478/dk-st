ARTIFICIAL LIFEFORM — Class "E"

Class "E" (also known as "Classie") is an artificial lifeform created by Dr. Ivo "Eggman" Robotnik for the purpose of finding a way out of the UNDERGROUND. Unfortunately for Eggman, using samples of Sonic's DETERMINATION to create an unfeeling artificial hedgehog didn't exactly work out, and now we have a genocidal Classic Sonic walking around, toying with lives and playing god until the day Cream returns.
