HONEY THE CAT

Honey is one of the more surprising additions to the DK!SONICTALE cast, as she is a character from Sonic the Fighters and the Archie Sonic the Hedgehog comics. Within this AU, she fulfills a dual role, being the closest parallel to both Grillby and Muffet at the same time. She owns an all-in-one parlor within the Ice Cap Forest. Despite her appearing like a side character, her connection to multiple main cast members puts her into a psuedo main character role.

Mischevious, a little bit self-serving, but a pillar of the community, Honey is the type of person who you NEED in a situation like this. She embodies community and the reconstruction of society in the face of great loss and trauma.
