YOU

You is the collective Determination of all real life humans. In simple terms, You is Our drive to create new stories. You exists solely to play the game, tell the story, read the story, write the story, and more. It is the ultimate amalgamation of every aspect of humanity, from the cruel and genocidal to the empathetic and pacifistic.

You is a very unique character within this story. You is not inherently violent, just as You are not inherently violent. You simply acts as a manifestation of every piece of humanity. You can think of You's mind as a metaphorical Twitch chat, constantly filtering through Itself to find the proper words It deems right for any given scenario.

Ultimately, It is beholden to the writers of the story Its fictional form resides within. As a result, despite Its knowledge of all of fiction, You is not able to voice everything.

"You" is to DK!SONICTALE what Dr. W.D. Gaster is to DELTARUNE. Incredibly important, but only ever surfacing at the start and end of the story.

To explain the origin of You's fictional vessel, it is important to state the theory that is being worked off of. This theory is that the MASTER EMERALD is the "system" in which the whole of humanity interfaces with the Sonic the Hedgehog franchise.

The only real evidence for this is how the MASTER EMERALD drew the CHAOS EMERALDS to Earth during a flashback in Sonic Frontiers, but using this as a jumping off point, the concept for You was created.

When Cream was protected by the MASTER EMERALD's energy, she was effectively linked with the very interface that We use to interact with the franchise. As a result, when Cream woke up six years later with no memories of what happened, You was dormant inside of her. You is the interface between fiction and reality manifested, and is the collective voice of every single human being that has ever touched upon fiction... ESPECIALLY Sonic the Hedgehog and Undertale related fiction.

It is because of this, that when Cream gathers more and more CHAOS EMERALDS, You gains more and more power and control over Cream's body. Eventually, once Cream has collected all 7, You is able to take full control and see the selected route (either Pacifist or Genocide/No Mercy) to its fullest conclusion.
