SONIC THE HEDGEHOG

Sonic the Hedgehog is DK!SONICTALE's closest parallel to Sans the Skeleton. The discoveries made, alongside the bleak situation they're in, it was all just too much for him. As a result, he grew discouraged. Giving up on escape, Sonic began to patrol the icy forest he called home, hoping that one day, they'll all find a savior.
